## Tips for Best Results

### Photo Quality

‚úÖ **Good photo**:
- Receipt flat on table
- Good lighting (no shadows)
- All text visible and sharp
- Entire receipt in frame
- Camera held steady

‚ùå **Bad photo**:
- Blurry or out of focus
- Receipt crumpled or folded
- Dark shadows or glare
- Text cut off
- Too far away (text too small)

### What We Check

Your receipt must:
- ‚úÖ Be from Lewis Retail (TIN: 0003169685)
- ‚úÖ Be from today (or within 24 hours)
- ‚úÖ Meet minimum purchase amount (usually 500 ETB)
- ‚úÖ Be from the correct branch
- ‚úÖ Not be a duplicate (already submitted)

## Common Issues

### "Receipt Not Eligible"

**Possible reasons**:
- Purchase amount too low
- Receipt is too old (not from today)
- Receipt already submitted
- Receipt not from Lewis Retail
- Wrong branch/location

**Solution**: Check your receipt and try with a valid one

### "Manual Review Needed"

**Possible reasons**:
- Photo is blurry or unclear
- System couldn't read some information
- Receipt format not recognized

**Solutions**:
- **Retake Photo**: Try again with better lighting
- **Submit for Review**: Let an admin verify it manually

### "Upload Failed"

**Possible reasons**:
- Internet connection issue
- File too large (> 8MB)
- Invalid file format
- Server temporary issue

**Solution**: Check connection and try again

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How long does verification take?**  
A: Usually 2-3 seconds for automatic verification.

**Q: What if my receipt is rejected?**  
A: You can retake the photo or try with a different valid receipt. Make sure it meets all requirements.

**Q: What happens with flagged receipts?**  
A: An admin will review it manually within 24 hours. You'll be notified of the decision.

**Q: Can I upload old receipts?**  
A: No, receipts must be from today (or within 24 hours depending on store policy).

**Q: Can I upload the same receipt twice?**  
A: No, each receipt can only be used once to earn a visit.

**Q: Do I get rewards?**  
A: Yes! Receipt uploads count as visits, and you earn rewards just like QR scans (every 5 visits = reward).

**Q: Is my receipt photo stored?**  
A: Yes, for verification purposes. We keep it secure and private.

**Q: Can I use QR and receipts?**  
A: Yes! Both methods work. Use whichever is more convenient.
